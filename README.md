EcoShop is a concept website that shows a sustainable living marketplace focused on eco-friendly products, education, and community engagement. The site is designed to show how users could explore environmentally safe products while learning ways to reduce waste and live more sustainably.

The website includes sections for browsing products, reading sustainability tips and blog content, estimating environmental impact through an interactive calculator, and engaging with a community forum. It also features areas for user accounts, a shopping cart experience, and informational pages such as FAQs, About, and Contact.

Overall, EcoShop demonstrates how a single website can bring together shopping, learning, and community around the goal of sustainable living.
